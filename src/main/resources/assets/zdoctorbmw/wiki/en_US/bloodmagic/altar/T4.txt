A Tier 4 altar needs an additional 28 Runes placed on the outskirts of a Tier 3 altar in a 7 x 7 fashion. Similarly to the Tier 3 Altar, 4 Large Stone Bricks  need to be placed 4 blocks above the corners (the pillars are purely decorational).
